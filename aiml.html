About the Department

The Department of Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning (AIML) at Walchand College of Engineering, Sangli was established in 2020 to meet the growing demand for AI professionals in India and globally. The department offers a 4-year B.Tech program with a total intake of 60 students per year.

Students are trained in cutting-edge areas including deep learning, natural language processing, computer vision, reinforcement learning, and data engineering. The curriculum is continuously updated in collaboration with industry partners to ensure graduates are industry-ready from day one.The Department of Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning at Walchand College of Engineering (WCE), Sangli, stands as a beacon of excellence, shaping the future of technology and innovation since its inception. Established in 2024, the department has consistently upheld its commitment to fostering technical expertise, research acumen, and ethical leadership in its students. The department has cultivated a reputation for academic rigor, blending foundational knowledge with cutting-edge technological advancements. With a carefully crafted curriculum aligned to industry needs and global standards, it prepares students to thrive in a rapidly evolving tech landscape. The emphasis on interdisciplinary learning, problem-solving, and real-world applications has made its graduates highly sought-after by top-tier organizations. Faculty and students alike have contributed to advancements in technology through publications in reputed journals, participation in conferences, and collaborative projects with industry leaders. The department maintains strong ties with the technology industry, enabling students to bridge the gap between academia and professional practice. Partnerships with renowned companies for internships, placements, and collaborative projects ensure that students gain hands-on experience and exposure to real-world challenges. The department boasts state-of-the-art infrastructure, including modern laboratories, high-performance computing facilities, and access to the latest software tools. These resources empower students to experiment, innovate, and excel in their endeavours. Additionally, a robust library and online resources provide the intellectual backbone for comprehensive learning. At the heart of the department is its student-centric approach. Faculty members serve as mentors, guiding students not just in academics but also in personal and professional development. The department actively promotes extracurricular activities, technical clubs, and community engagement, fostering a holistic growth environment.
