About Walchand College of Engineering

Established in 1947, Walchand College of Engineering, Sangli is a Government-Aided Autonomous Institute affiliated to Shivaji University, Kolhapur. It is one of the oldest and most prestigious engineering institutions in Maharashtra.

Spread over a 140-acre campus, WCE offers undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs across 8 engineering departments. The college is NBA Accredited and NAAC A+ Accredited.
